These are the steps to become a developer

These steps are key to becoming a developer: learn programming, practice, use Git, explore frameworks, and build a portfolio.

STEP 1

Begin with learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as they form the foundation of web development.

STEP 2

Practice regularly by building small projects, such as simple websites or applications, to reinforce your knowledge.

STEP 3

Learn version control with Git to track changes in your code and collaborate with others on projects.

STEP 4

Explore popular frameworks and libraries like React, Angular, or Django, to streamline development and add advanced features to your projects.

FINISH

Create a portfolio that showcases your best work, demonstrating your skills to potential employers or clients.
